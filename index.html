About Me

I am a dedicated Electronics and Communication Engineering student at Pandit Deendayal Energy University (PDEU), maintaining a CGPA of 8.07/10. My core passion lies at the intersection of complex digital hardware design and embedded artificial intelligence.

My expertise is heavily focused on System-on-Chip (SoC) architecture. I specialize in designing and verifying industry-standard bus protocols, specifically the AMBA AXI4-Full specification. By combining RTL design with rigorous SystemVerilog Object-Oriented verification (CRV and SVA), I ensure the creation of high-throughput, zero-defect silicon.

Beyond the register-transfer level, I engineer end-to-end Edge-AI solutions. I design firmware and quantize neural networks (TinyML) to deploy predictive maintenance diagnostics directly onto resource-constrained microcontrollers, eliminating the need for cloud compute in industrial environments.
